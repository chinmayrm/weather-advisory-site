How to Use This Site
Get your weather-based crop advisory in 4 easy steps:
Step 1: Select your crop from the popular crops or dropdown menu.
Step 2: Enter your city and choose the season and soil type.
Step 3: Click Get Advisory to receive AI-powered, crop-specific weather recommendations.
Step 4: Review, copy, share, or save the advisory for your records.
Tip: Our system uses real-time weather and crop data to give you actionable, point-wise advice for your farm. For best results, enter accurate details!

About Agri AI

Agri AI Weather Advisory is a smart farming tool designed to help farmers make informed decisions based on current weather conditions and crop requirements. Our system combines real-time weather data with agricultural expertise to provide actionable recommendations.

This service is particularly valuable for small and medium-scale farmers who need accurate, localized advice to optimize their farming practices and maximize yields.
