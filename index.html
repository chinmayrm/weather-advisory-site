About Agri AI

Agri AI Weather Advisory is a smart farming tool designed to help farmers make informed decisions based on current weather conditions and crop requirements. Our system combines real-time weather data with agricultural expertise to provide actionable recommendations.

This service is particularly valuable for small and medium-scale farmers who need accurate, localized advice to optimize their farming practices and maximize yields.
